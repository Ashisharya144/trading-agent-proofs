Under construction · building in public, cryptographically

An autonomous AI trading system that will prove —
not claim — every result.

Trading products all say the same thing: "trust our returns." We're building the opposite: a system whose entire decision history is sealed with public cryptographic proofs, every single day — so when the books open, nothing can have been edited, cherry-picked, or invented. Predictions provably made before outcomes. Verifiable by anyone.

What already exists (and is provably running)

  • A committee of AI analysts that debates every decision — and an immutable risk constitution no AI can override
  • Analysts earn influence through graded accuracy; new ones train in shadow with zero power until evidence promotes them
  • Every rejected trade is tracked too — the system's judgment is audited, not just its wins
  • A nightly adversarial AI auditor that hunts for the system's own flaws
  • Daily public proof: a SHA-256 fingerprint of the day's full records, published here every close
  • The evaluation's pass/fail rules — pre-registered and sealed before the results exist

What happens next

A 30-trading-day sealed evaluation is running now. In late August 2026, the books open — every decision, every trade, every loss, judged against pre-registered criteria and honest baselines, verifiable against the fingerprints already published below your feet.

How it works

Three ideas. No trust required.

01

A committee, not a bot

Multiple AI analysts covering price, news, and the economy debate every trade. Analysts earn influence by being right and lose it by being wrong. New ones train with zero power until evidence promotes them. Above them all: risk rules no AI can override.

02

Sealed in public, daily

Every evening, a cryptographic fingerprint of the day's full records is published to a public repository. It reveals no strategy, but it makes the history impossible to edit later. Predictions provably came before outcomes.

03

The books open. Fully.

In late August the complete record is revealed: every trade, including the losses, judged against pass/fail criteria that were locked in before the results existed, and benchmarked against the market itself. Anyone can verify it matches the seals.

The evaluation

30 trading days. Rules fixed in advance.

No cherry-picking, no moving goalposts. The success criteria were committed publicly before day one's results, including the choices that currently make our numbers look worse. That's the point.
